## ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ³ Preparation

1. Mix mashed potatoes with achiote powder, salt, and pepper.
2. Form potato mixture into small patties.
3. Place cheese in center of each patty and seal.
4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
5. Cook patties until golden brown on both sides.
6. Serve hot with peanut sauce.
