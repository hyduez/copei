This classic Italian bruschetta is a simple yet flavorful appetizer made with toasted artisan bread, fresh tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil. Perfect for a quick snack or as a starter for any meal.

Its charm lies in the freshness of the ingredients and the balance between crunchy bread and juicy tomatoes.

- 2 slices of artisan or rustic bread

- 1 clove of garlic  
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil  
- Salt, to taste  
- Fresh basil leaves (optional)  

---

## ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ³ Preparation

1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
2. Cut the garlic clove in half and gently rub it over the warm toast.
3. Dice the tomatoes into small cubes and place them in a bowl.
4. Add olive oil and salt to the tomatoes, mixing gently.
5. Spoon the tomato mixture over the toasted bread.
6. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
