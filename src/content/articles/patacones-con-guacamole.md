## ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ³ Preparation

1. Peel green plantains and cut into 1-inch thick rounds.
2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
3. Fry plantain rounds for 3-4 minutes until golden.
4. Remove and flatten each piece with a tostonera or heavy plate.
5. Return flattened plantains to oil and fry until crispy.
6. Season with salt and top with desired toppings.
