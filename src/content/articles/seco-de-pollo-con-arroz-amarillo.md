Slow-cooked chicken in beer and cilantro sauce served with Arroz Amarillo.

This slow-cooked stew gets its distinctive flavor from cilantro and beer, creating a rich, complex sauce.

- 1/2 cup beer  
- 2 tablespoons oil  
- Salt, pepper, cumin to taste  
- Arroz ingredients (rice or lentils)
