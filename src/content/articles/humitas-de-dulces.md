Traditional Ecuadorian steamed corn cakes with sweet corn.

These steamed corn cakes are a traditional Andean delicacy, enjoyed across Ecuador for generations.

- 1-2 cups dulces mixture

- 6 fresh ears of corn, kernels removed  
- 1/2 cup cornmeal  
- 1/4 cup butter  
- 1 egg  
- 1/2 cup milk  
- 1 teaspoon baking powder  
- Salt to taste  
- Corn husks for wrapping  

---

## ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ³ Preparation

1. Blend corn kernels with butter until smooth paste forms.
2. Mix in cornmeal, egg, milk, baking powder, and salt.
3. Soak corn husks in warm water to soften.
4. Place mixture in center of each husk and wrap tightly.
5. Steam for 45-60 minutes until firm.
6. Let cool slightly before serving.
