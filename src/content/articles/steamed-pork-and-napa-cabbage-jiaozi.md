## ğŸ¥Ÿ Steamed Pork and Napa Cabbage Jiaozi

Jiaozi are traditional Chinese dumplings known for their delicate wrappers and flavorful fillings. This steamed version combines lean pork with napa cabbage, creating a light yet satisfying dish that is both nutritious and comforting.

Steaming preserves the natural flavors and keeps the dumplings tender, making them a healthier alternative to pan-fried versions.
